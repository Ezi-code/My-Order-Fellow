My Order Fellow
Project Overview
My Order Fellow is a SaaS application designed to help e-commerce companies provide
real-time order tracking updates to their customers.
The platform integrates with external e-commerce applications via webhooks. Once an order is
received, My Order Fellow manages tracking subscriptions, listens for status updates from the
e-commerce platform, and relays those updates to end users through notifications.

User Roles
1.​ E-commerce Company (B2B Client)
○​ Registers and manages an account
○​ Integrates their platform via webhook
○​ Sends order and tracking updates
2.​ End Customer (Order Owner)
○​ Receives tracking notifications via email
Functional Requirements
1. Authentication & Onboarding
E-commerce Company Registration
●​ Company creates an account on My Order Fellow
●​ Required fields:
○​ Company name○​ Business email
○​ Password
Email Verification
●​ System sends a 6-digit OTP to the registered email
●​ Account is activated after OTP verification
KYC Process
●​ Company submits KYC information:
○​ Business registration number
○​ Business address
○​ Contact person details
●​ Admin reviews and approves/rejects KYC
●​ Only approved companies can activate webhooks
2. Order Intake & Tracking Process
Order Reception (Webhook-Based)
●​ Registered e-commerce companies send order data to My Order Fellow via webhook
(mock)
●​ Sample order data may include:
○​ Order ID
○​ Customer email / phone
○​ Item summary
○​ Delivery address
○​ Initial tracking status
Tracking Subscription Initialization​
Once an order is received:
●​ The order is stored in the system
●​ Tracking is automatically activated
●​ A confirmation notification is sent to the end customer
3. Tracking Status Updates
Listening for Updates
●​ My Order Fellow continuously listens for tracking updates from the e-commerce platform●​ Updates are received via webhook events
Supported Status Flow​
Each order can transition through the following statuses:
●​ PENDING
●​ IN TRANSIT
●​ OUT FOR DELIVERY
●​ DELIVERED
Status Update Rules
●​ Each update may include:
○​ New status
○​ Timestamp
○​ Optional additional note (e.g., "Package arrived at Lagos hub")
●​ Status history must be stored for audit and display
4. Notifications
Customer Notifications
●​ Notification is sent when:
○​ Tracking is successfully activated
○​ Order status changes
Notification Channels
●​ Email notifications
